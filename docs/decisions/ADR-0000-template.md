# ADR-0000 — Title in one sentence

critique finding (with anchor) or RFC. Three to six sentences.

## Decision

Optionally a short bulleted list of constraints we accept.

## Consequences

CI, what we now have to maintain, what we explicitly stop doing.
Three to six bullets.
